Fiona Davis is the New York Times bestselling author of several novels,including The Dollhouse, The Address, The Masterpiece, The Chelsea Girls, The Lions of Fifth Avenue, and The Magnolia Palace. She lives in New York City and is a graduate of the Columbia Journalism School.Fiona Davis was born in Canada and raised in New Jersey, Utah, and Texas. She began her career in New York City as an actress, where she worked on Broadway, off-Broadway, and in regional theater. After ten years, she changed careers, working as an editor and writer and specializing in health, fitness, nutrition, dance, and theater.She is a graduate of the College of William and Mary and the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism, and is based in in New York City.
